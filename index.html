ABOUT ME

Web Developer

I would describe myself as a creative thinker. Someone who enjoys a challenge or puzzle. But also someone who wants to make things known in a way for people to see and understand clearly. Which is why I chose to be a front end developer. My specialties include HTML, CSS, Javascript, and ReactJS. I also have a basic understanding on SQLLite and PostgresSQL along with Ruby on Rails.

Dominique Brisco


Formerly a collegiate track and field athlete, I graduated from the University of Houston with a Bachelor of Science in Human Resource Development and a Master of Arts in Sports and Fitness Administration. I spent the first few years of "the real world" working in insurance, but in my free time I often found time to use the computer to create things. Whether I was using the computer to create a website on Wix or using Adobe to create graphics and videos for my church... my love for technology and visuals often merged.

During the COVID-19 pandemic, I had more time on my hands and seriously considered how to enter the tech field. It was something I had wanted to do since high school after taking a web design course. While in college, my track schedule did not allow for the design courses so I decided to choose another field. After graduating, I tried the self taught route, and even pursued a CS degree, before ultimately sitting down during the pandemic and figuring out that a bootcamp was the right track for me. I quit my job two months prior to bootcamp to focus on learning the basics of Ruby, by October I began Flatiron School (Houston but remote due to COVID-19) learning Ruby on Rails, SQL, Javascript, and React.
